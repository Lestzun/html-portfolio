The Best Movies According to Lester

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Fury

Great war movie about a tank crew in WWII Europe

Never Back Down

This movie is about a guy that gets beat on but trains to the point where he is the best fighter and competes in a brawling tournament.

Dune

One of the best movies to come out this year. Has quality, visuals and a great story line.
